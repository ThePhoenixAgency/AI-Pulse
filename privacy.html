Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2026

At AI-Pulse, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal, anonymized analytics data to improve our service:

  • Anonymous visitor statistics: We track page views, session duration, and navigation patterns
  • Location data: We collect anonymized city and country information (not precise GPS)
  • Technical data: Browser type, device type, and screen resolution
  • Cookies: We use session cookies for analytics purposes only

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data exclusively for:

  • Understanding website traffic and user behavior
  • Improving user experience and website performance
  • Generating aggregate statistics

We do NOT:

  • Sell or share your data with third parties
  • Track you across other websites
  • Collect personally identifiable information (PII)
  • Use advertising or marketing trackers

3. GDPR Compliance

AI-Pulse is fully compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Data Minimization: We only collect essential analytics data
  • Anonymization: All IP addresses are anonymized
  • Consent: We obtain cookie consent before tracking
  • Right to Access: You can request access to any data we have
  • Right to Deletion: You can request deletion of your data at any time
  • Cookie Duration: Analytics cookies expire after 13 months (GDPR compliant)

4. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies to prevent duplicate visitor counts
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site (13-month expiry)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security audits
  • No storage of sensitive personal information

6. Third-Party Services

AI-Pulse does not use any third-party analytics or advertising services. All analytics are processed locally and stored securely.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service does not target children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

  • GitHub: Open an issue
  • Organization: ThePhoenixAgency

10. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request data deletion
  • Object to data processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time
