Frequently asked questions

How much does it cost?

Jack is free and open source. You only pay for your server (Railway free tier works) and AI API usage (typically $5-20/month for a small hotel).

What AI providers are supported?

Claude (Anthropic), GPT (OpenAI), Ollama for local models, and built-in local AI using Transformers.js. Mix and match as needed.

Can I run it without cloud AI?

Yes! Jack includes local AI models that run entirely on your server. No external API calls required for basic functionality.

How do I connect WhatsApp?

Through WhatsApp Business API (Meta). The setup wizard guides you through the process. SMS works via Twilio, email via any SMTP.

Is my data secure?

Your data stays on your server. We never see it. All credentials are encrypted at rest. You control backups and access.
