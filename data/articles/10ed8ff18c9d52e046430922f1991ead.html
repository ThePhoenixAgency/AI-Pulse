Wildex - Discover Wildlife Around You Turn every walk into a treasure hunt. Wildex uses your camera to instantly identify any plant or animal, then adds it to your personal collection—like Pokémon Go, but everything is real. [Collect] • Point your camera at any living thing—get an instant ID with species info • Every species has a rarity tier based on how common it is in your area • Build a beautiful visual collection of everything you've found • That weird bird at the park? Now it's yours forever [Discover Your Area] • See which species have been found near you—and which haven't • Hidden legendary species are waiting in your neighborhood • Your local park has dozens of species you've walked past without noticing • Track where and when you found each one on your personal map [Compete] • Climb local and global leaderboards • Complete quests for bonus XP • Rare finds = massive points • See how your collection stacks up against friends • Share finds with one tap [Learn] • Every species comes with facts and info • Actually remember what you saw on that hike 100% free. Works anywhere there's nature—your backyard counts! Lots of love, The Wildex Team
