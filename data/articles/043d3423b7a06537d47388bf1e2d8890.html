Your app got crashed.Your team already knows.

Yeethook catches App Store Connect webhooks and App Store Server Notifications, enriches them with full context using your p8 key, and routes the complete picture to the right Slack channels. Crash logs, tester names, screenshots, subscription data. Upload your p8 key and go, or configure manually.

How it works

Four steps, done.

1

Connect Apple

Upload your p8 key for automatic setup, or choose Manual to configure webhooks yourself.

2

Add your apps

Pick your apps and choose which event types to receive. Quick Start imports them from App Store Connect.

3

Connect Slack

One-click Slack app creation from a pre-built manifest. Link apps to channels and route events where they belong.

4

Done

Events start flowing. Yeethook monitors webhook health on both sides and auto-repairs if anything breaks.

One service, every event

Formatted by default. Enriched with your p8 key. Every Apple event arrives in Slack as a readable message. Add your p8 key and we'll attach the details Apple doesn't include.

Subscription events

Renewals, cancellations, refunds, grace periods. Every App Store Server Notification type, covered.

Crash reports

Full crash logs, tester name, device model, OS version. All in one Slack message.

TestFlight feedback

Screenshots and comments from testers, delivered with device details.

Webhook health & repair

Yeethook monitors every webhook on both sides. Deleted? Recreated. Disabled? Re-enabled. URL drifted? Fixed.

Connect your apps in seconds.

Upload your p8 key and Yeethook auto-imports every app from App Store Connect. Pick which ones to monitor, choose your event types, and go. No manual webhook setup required.
