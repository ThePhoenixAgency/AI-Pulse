The Orico MiniPro Dock Case does turn your Mac mini into a cute mini Mac Pro replica, but it's not just the small size that's limiting. Orico MiniPro Dock Case review: A miniaturized Mac Pro case for a Mac mini The current generation of Mac mini has become a favorite among enclosure designers, since it can easily be placed inside a casing and made to look like something else. Sometimes, this can look like other retro computers, and do so with great effect. In the case of the Orico MiniPro Dock Case, the intention was to create something that borrowed its appearance from something historically associated with performance.
