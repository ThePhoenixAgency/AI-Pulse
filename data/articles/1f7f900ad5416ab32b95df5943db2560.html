macOS Menu Bar App

Never miss a meeting again.

Galopen lives in your macOS menu bar, showing upcoming calendar events and automatically opening meeting URLs right when you need them.

Download for macOSView on GitHub

Features

Menu Bar Native

Lives quietly in your menu bar. Click to see today's and tomorrow's schedule at a glance.

Auto-Open Meetings

Automatically opens Zoom, Google Meet, and Teams links at a configurable time before the meeting starts.

Countdown Timer

See how many minutes until your next meeting, right in the menu bar. Configurable display threshold.

Calendar Filtering

Toggle visibility of individual calendars. Works with all calendars synced to your Mac.

Privacy First

All data stays on your Mac. No accounts, no servers, no tracking. Uses the native macOS EventKit.

Lightweight

Built with Tauri for minimal resource usage. Small download, fast startup, low memory footprint.

Get started in seconds.

Download Galopen, grant calendar access, and you're good to go. Free and open source.
