Philosophy

Not another dashboard.

Mixpanel, Amplitude, PostHog — they give you charts and expect you to figure out the rest. They were built for companies with data teams. You don't have a data team. You have a laptop and a deadline.

Lurnna is opinionated by design. It doesn't give you 47 chart types and a query builder. It gives you 3–5 things to do today that will move the needle. Every insight is specific (real numbers), actionable (tells you what to do), and non-obvious (something you wouldn't have noticed).

If you want to build custom funnels and write SQL, use PostHog. If you want someone to tell you why your users aren't coming back and what to do about it, use Lurnna.
