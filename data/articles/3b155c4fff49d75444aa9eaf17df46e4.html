SSL Certificate Monitor

Free · In‑App Purchases · Designed for iPad

Monitor SSL certificate expiration dates for your domains with a clean, native iOS app. No accounts, no subscriptions — just clarity. CertWarden helps you monitor SSL certificate expiration dates for your domains — simply, clearly, and without accounts. Add your domains and instantly see certificate status and expiration dates in a clean, card-based interface. CertWarden is designed for developers, DevOps engineers, website owners, and anyone who wants to avoid unexpected certificate expirations. KEY FEATURES • SSL certificate expiration monitoring • Clear status indicators (valid, expiring, expired) • Group domains by project or environment • Manual certificate checks • Detailed certificate information view • Pull-to-refresh and swipe actions • Full Dark Mode support • Native iOS design with SwiftUI • Optional webhook notifications for certificate status changes • API access for advanced and external integrations (Pro) NO ACCOUNTS REQUIRED CertWarden does not require sign-in. An anonymous device-based identifier is used only to associate domains with the device. No personal data is collected. OPTIONAL PRO FEATURES Unlock Pro features with a one-time purchase. • Unlimited domains and groups • Webhook notifications with multiple endpoints • Priority support • Lifetime access No subscriptions. No recurring payments. CertWarden focuses on doing one thing well: helping you stay ahead of SSL certificate expirations. Webhook and API features are optional and intended for advanced users.
