I Built 8 Free Tools Nobody Asked For (And Why They Cost $0)

Source: Dev.to Open Source | Date: 2/18/2026 4:11:07 AM | Lien | Lang: EN

I spent the last few months building free tools that solve actual problems. Not SaaS tools with a paywall after 3 uses. Actually free. Here is what I built and why.

The Tools

VIN Decoder - Paste any 17-digit Vehicle Identification Number and get instant results. Year, make, model, engine, safety features. Uses the official NHTSA database. Try it: simplylouie.com/vin-decoder

Auto Loan Calculator - Enter price, down payment, rate, and term. Get your monthly payment instantly. No email required, no upsell. simplylouie.com/loan-calculator

Solar ROI Calculator - Enter your electric bill and see your estimated system size, federal tax credit (30%), payback period, and 25-year savings. simplylouie.com/solar-calculator

Home Value Estimator - Get a quick property value estimate based on address, square footage, and comparable sales data.

AI Text Summarizer - Paste any text and get a clean summary. Powered by Claude AI.

AI Email Writer - Describe what you need and get a professional email draft.

Business Name Generator - Enter your industry and get 10 creative business name ideas.

Translator - Translate between any languages including Pidgin, Tagalog, Bahasa, and Swahili.

Why Free?

Because I am tired of tools that bait you with "free" and then hit you with a paywall. These tools cost me almost nothing to run. The VIN decoder uses the free NHTSA API. The calculators run in your browser with zero server cost.

The AI tools use a lightweight model that costs fractions of a penny per use. I can afford to give them away.

The Tech Stack

  • Node.js + Express backend
  • SQLite for data
  • Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS frontend (no React, no build step)
  • NHTSA API for VIN data
  • Claude Haiku for AI features
  • Hosted on a single DigitalOcean droplet

What I Learned

  1. Free tools are the best marketing. People searching "free VIN decoder" have real intent. They need something solved right now.

  2. No-signup tools get shared. When something just works without asking for your email, people bookmark it and tell friends.

  3. Browser-side calculation is underrated. The loan and solar calculators run 100% client-side. Zero latency, zero cost.

  4. Simple beats complex. No framework, no build step, no dependencies beyond what is needed. The VIN decoder page is one HTML file.

All the tools are at simplylouie.com/tools. They are part of a larger project to make AI accessible for $2/month, but the tools are free forever regardless.
