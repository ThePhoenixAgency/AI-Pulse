More Total War games arrive on GOG along with a return of The Long Dark

Source: GamingOnLinux | Date: 2/12/2026 | Lang: EN | Original Article

GOG has more goodies available for fans of DRM-free gaming with the release of more Total War games, and The Long Dark has returned as well.

The Long Dark is an interesting one, as it left the GOG store back in 2018 but it has now returned. It comes split into multiple versions between the survival mode and the story mode, or there's a bundle available with everything. Hinterland said that if you already had it on GOG from before you should get the new versions automatically.

In related sad news, the developer Hinterland also just announced some layoffs along with The Long Dark sequel taking longer than expected.

In other GOG news they've also gained more Total War games. Late last year saw a few classics arrive on GOG, and then Total War: THREE KINGDOMS as well. Now the new additions include:

  • Total War: PHARAOH DYNASTIES
  • Total War: ROME II - Emperor Edition
  • Total War: ATTILA

Each arrive with their DLC and they're also on sale.
