Apple Books and Apple TV Celebrate “The Last Thing He Told Me” Season 2 Premiere

Source: The Mac Observer | Date: 2/14/2026 | Lang: EN

Apple TV and Apple Books marked the return of The Last Thing He Told Me with a live event in Los Angeles as season two prepares to launch globally. The new season premieres Friday, February 20, with fresh episodes arriving every Friday through April 10. The series builds on the suspense and family drama that defined its first run, this time raising the stakes for Hannah and Bailey.

The event took place on February 12 at The West Hollywood EDITION. Executive producer Reese Witherspoon joined star and executive producer Jennifer Garner, along with cast members Angourie Rice, Judy Greer, and Rita Wilson. Author and co-creator Laura Dave also attended. They recorded a live episode of the “Bookmarked” podcast from Reese’s Book Club, moderated by journalist Danielle Robay.

According to Apple Newsroom, the second season adapts Dave’s instant New York Times bestselling sequel novel, The First Time I Saw Him. In this chapter, Owen returns after five years on the run. Hannah and her stepdaughter Bailey must confront the past and race to protect their family before old secrets resurface.

Story, Cast, and Release Schedule

Season two opens with the first episode on February 20, followed by weekly releases:

  • Premiere date: February 20, 2026
  • New episodes: Every Friday
  • Season finale: April 10, 2026

The returning and new cast includes David Morse, Augusto Aguilera, Josh Hamilton, Nick Hargrove, Michael Galante, John Noble, Michael Hyatt, Luke Kirby, and Elizabeth O’Donnell. The series comes from 20th Television and Witherspoon’s Hello Sunshine, part of Candle Media. Dave created and adapted the show alongside co-creator Josh Singer, with Aaron Zelman joining as co-showrunner for season two.

Ahead of the premiere, readers can explore “The First Time I Saw Him” on Apple Books in print, ebook or audiobook format. The platform remains the official home for Reese’s Book Club selections and regularly features monthly picks for its readers.

With a global launch set for February 20, Apple TV positions season two of “The Last Thing He Told Me” as one of its major early 2026 releases.
