TapPause is a simple space to stop for a moment. No tasks. No tracking. No goals to complete. Just a pause. Tap the button. Let the time pass. You don’t have to do anything. Designed to be quiet, intentional, and distraction-free. TapPause helps you step out of momentum — even briefly. Features: • A single pause button • Adjustable pause duration • Optional sound and haptics • Landscape mode support • No accounts, no data collection Free includes a simple 5-minute pause. Unlock 1–30 minute pauses with a one-time purchase. TapPause is not a productivity tool. It’s a reminder that you’re allowed to stop.
