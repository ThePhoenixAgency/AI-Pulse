4 great soundbars in the Presidents' Day sales that will instantly boost your TVs audio game — but will keep…

Source: TechRadar | Date: 2/16/2026 6:31:52 PM | Lang: EN | Original Article

One of the best upgrades you can make to your TV setup is to add a soundbar to your system. Most TVs are a bit disappointing in the audio department, and that's because of their slim chassis: there just isn't much room for speakers. That's why we've chosen four Best Buy soundbar deals that are definitely worth checking out among the Presidents' Day sales: you don't need to spend too much money to make a really big difference.

The simplest, most compact soundbars, such as the LG S20A soundbar for $99, are twin-channel, adding extra oomph to your TV's sound. But as you go up the price range you get more speakers delivering more audio channels – five channels of immersive Dolby Atmos on the Sonos Beam (Gen 2) for $369, or you can get 5.1.1 channels with rear speakers and a subwoofer on the LG S70TR for $349.
