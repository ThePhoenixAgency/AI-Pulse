iOS 26.4 beta adds new ‘Playlist Playground’ AI feature for Apple Music

Source: 9to5Mac | Date: 2/16/2026 8:18:23 PM | Lang: EN | Original Article

Apple released the first beta of iOS 26.4 for developers today, and it includes a new Apple Intelligence feature for Apple Music.

Dubbed “Playlist Playground,” this feature lets you create playlists based on text descriptions.

This feature is labeled as a “beta” for now. You can access it in the Apple Music app by tapping the new playlist icon in the upper-right corner of the “Library” page.

You can then simply enter a text prompt and Apple Music will generate a playlist based on your request. There are also built-in “New Playlist Ideas” prompts as well.

iOS 26.4 is available to developer beta testers. A public beta is expected within the next several weeks.
