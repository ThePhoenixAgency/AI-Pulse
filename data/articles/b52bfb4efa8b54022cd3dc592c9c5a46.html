Apple Creator Studio users are hitting generative AI usage limits far too soon

Source: AppleInsider | Date: 2/12/2026 1:34:18 PM | Lien | Lang: EN

Apple buries the fact that its Apple Creator Studio bundle's generative AI features come with any usage limits, but the limits are real and now appear to be significantly less than expected. You can check your AI feature usage in the iWorks app — if you even realise that there are any limits. Apple Creator Studio is a bundle of apps such as Final Cut Pro and Logic Pro, plus updated versions of the iWork ones such as Pages and Keynote. In each case, Apple heavily promotes how the apps all come with new Apple Intelligence features. Apple also promotes the bundle as meaning "endless creativity... unlimited possibilities," but those AI features are in fact limited.
