CyberGame 2026

Source: CTFtime | Date: Invalid Date | Lang: EN | Original Article

CyberGame is the Slovak national cybersecurity competition organized by the National Cyber Security Centre SK-CERT.

The competition is an individual event spanning 10 weeks, running from March 1 (00:00 CET) to May 9 (23:59 CET). It also serves as the official Slovak qualifier for the European Cyber Security Challenge (ECSC).

Challenges cover a variety of categories - including Cryptography, OSINT, Forensics, Malware Analysis, Offensive Security, and more - with tasks ranging from beginner to advanced difficulty.
