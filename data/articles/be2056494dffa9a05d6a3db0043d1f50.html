Make your job hunt easy by automating your application process with this Auto Applier LinkedIn AI Auto Job Applier This is an web scraping bot that automates the process of job applications on LinkedIn. It searches for jobs relevant to you, answers all questions in application form, customizes your resume based on the collected job information, such as skills required, description, about company, etc. and applies to the job. Can apply 100+ jobs in less than 1 hour.
