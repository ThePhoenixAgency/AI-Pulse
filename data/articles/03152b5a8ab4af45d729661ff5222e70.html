Source: Hacker News Show | Date: 2/18/2026 6:35:33 AM | Lien | Lang: EN

I built a pipeline for querying fleet telemetry (AVs, robots, vehicles) in natural language. Load Parquet or CSV into cuDF, then ask things like "Which vehicles exceeded 120 km/h in region X?" and get back IDs and metrics. Tech: cuDF for GPU ingest/analytics, NVIDIA NIM on GKE for LLM inference, format-aware model selection (GGUF local, TensorRT prod). Three notebooks: data ingest with pandas vs cuDF vs cudf.pandas benchmarks, local Gemma 2 inference, and full NIM deployment.
