Cybersecurity threats seem to be more prominent and advanced than ever before, with average users having no chance of keeping themselves protected. Thankfully, at times like this, we can turn to the Moonlock app developed by MacPaw.

The specialized cybersecurity app is designed specifically to protect macOS from advanced threats by providing malware detection, real-time protection, and digital privacy.

Save 20% on Moonlock security

Moonlock is a user-friendly app that provides deep, under-the-hood security. It goes beyond basic scans and checks areas often overlooked by standard tools, such as external drives, mail attachments, and archived files. This is proactive defense at its best.

Unlike many generic antivirus programs, Moonlock is built natively for macOS. It allows users to perform basic to deep scans which identify potential malware and even hidden unwanted programs.

Beyond scanning for viruses, Moonlock functions as a broader privacy advocate for the Mac ecosystem. It operates a specialized research lab that monitors emerging Mac-specific vulnerabilities and shares insights with the cybersecurity community.

Its feature set also includes a built-in VPN for secure browsing and a unique Network Inspector that allows users to monitor and block data traffic to specific geographic regions. It also helps users optimize their Mac’s native settings like FileVault and Firewall to ensure the entire system is hardened against attacks.
