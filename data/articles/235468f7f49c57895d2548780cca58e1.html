Your feed used to be about work.

Shipping products, closing deals, changing careers, building things that matter. Somewhere along the way it became a stream of AI hot takes, GPT screenshots, and thought leaders discovering ChatGPT for the first time.

Pre-AI doesn't use an algorithm. It reads every post in your feed and sorts them into two buckets: AI content and everything else. One toggle. You decide what you see.

01

Real-time scanning

Every post is classified as it loads, including infinite scroll. No delay, no page refresh.

02

Intelligent detection

Multi-tier classification using brand names, technical phrases, hashtags, and context-aware pattern matching.

03

Three modes

Pre-AI hides AI content. AI Only shows nothing but. All mode shows everything with subtle markers.

Nothing leaves your browser.

No backend. No analytics. No tracking. No account. The extension runs entirely on your machine. Your feed data stays yours because we never see it.
