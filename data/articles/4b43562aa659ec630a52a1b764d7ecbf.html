ROLLIN is America's wheelchair accessibility map. We help people with mobility challenges find restaurants, bars, and cafes that are actually accessible.

Across 6 states and 22 regions, we provide real accessibility data — wheelchair entries, accessible restrooms, parking, elevators, and more. Verified by the community, not a checkbox.
