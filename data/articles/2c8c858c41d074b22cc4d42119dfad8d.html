'Tehran' producer Dana Eden dies during filming

Source: AppleInsider | Date: 2/16/2026 2:10:30 PM | Lien | Lang: EN

Dana Eden, a producer working on the Apple TV espionage drama "Tehran," has been found dead while filming for the show in Greece. Dana Eden at the second-season premiere of 'Tehran' - Image Credit: Apple Filming of the fourth season of spy thriller Tehran was underway when Eden was discovered in an Athens hotel on Sunday. Discovered by her brother, Eden's death is being treated as a suicide initially, with an investigation underway to determine what happened to her. Israeli public broadcaster Kan, which produces the show along with Apple TV, called Eden a senior figure in Israel's television industry, reports Deadline.
