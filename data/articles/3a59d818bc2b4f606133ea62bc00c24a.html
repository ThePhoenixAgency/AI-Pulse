A streamlined workflow designed to take you from back-of-napkin sketch to high-fidelity designs.

Step 01

Brainstorm & Scope

Start by explaining your initial idea. Work through a dynamic questionnaire to refine your vision. Mowgli will translate this to your unique specification.

Step 02

The Spec View

Get a god-mode view of your application. Iterate on the spec directly to refine requirements before pixel-pushing.

Step 03

Generate Designs

Pick a theme, steer the style, or remix until it's perfect. Mowgli generates high-fidelity designs for every screen and state. Not just happy paths - explore all nooks and crannies of your product.

Step 04

Ideate by Design

Meet your project and ideate quickly by design. Use natural language to iterate on features, update flows, or polish UI details in real-time.

Step 05

Export

Ready to build? Get a curated master prompt to continue in your AI-powered builder, export into Figma or download a .zip with your spec and React code and get hands-on yourself.

key features

Everything you need to go from idea to production-ready design.

AI Design Generation

Generate complete, high-fidelity UI designs for your whole app or feature idea in seconds and iterate on it with a powerful AI assistant. Mowgli explores your product in depth and designs every possible screen, flow, and state.

Figma Import

Bring your existing Figma designs into Mowgli and use chat to edit, iterate, and evolve them. Tweak layouts, update copy, restyle pages, or rethink whole flows - all with AI. When you're done, export to code for building, or back to Figma for tweaks.

Chat with Your Design

Select any element and say what to change. Mowgli edits your design through conversation - targeted tweaks or sweeping changes, all in plain English.

Export to Code

Export your AI-generated or Figma designs as pixel-perfect React components, Figma files, or AI-ready packages with curated prompts for Claude and Cursor.

Style Steering

Steer your AI-generated designs with color palettes and style descriptions. Get themed, consistent designs that match your brand without manual styling.

Responsive Adaptation

Switch between mobile and desktop and Mowgli reimagines your design from the ground up for the target platform.
