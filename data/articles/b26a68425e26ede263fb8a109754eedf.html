EHAX CTF 2026

Source: CTFtime | Date: Invalid Date | Lang: EN | Original Article

EHAX CTF 2026 is a 36 hour long jeopardy style capture the flag competition.
Team size: no limit

The challenges cover various topics such as: Cryptography, Web Security, Reverse Engineering, Binary Exploitation, Forensics, AI/ML security and IOT security. There is a flag associated to each level, players have to solve the challenges and submit the flags to reach the top of the scoreboard.
