This week on Paris des Arts, Valérie Fayolle sits down with actor and comedian Michel Boujenah. He's currently starring in "Toute la famille que j'aime" ("The whole family I love"), a witty comedy at the Théâtre des Variétés. Lilly Wood & the Prick also feature in this edition as they return with a fifth album "Christina", 10 years after the worldwide success of "Prayer in C".

Plus, we catch a glimpse of vintage prints of the late Brigitte Bardot at the Galerie de l'Instant. It's a retrospective dedicated to the "BB" of the 1950s and 60s, who revolutionised the concept of femininity.
