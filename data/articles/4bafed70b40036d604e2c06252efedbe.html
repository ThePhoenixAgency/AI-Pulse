Husky gatecrashes team sprint race to pip Olympians at the finish line

Source: France 24 (EN) | Date: 2/18/2026 4:55:50 PM | Lien | Lang: EN

A husky with lots of enthusiasm ​but no credentials gatecrashed a women's team sprint cross-country race at the Winter Olympics 2026 event on Wednesday, giving an impromptu performance that had spectators howling with delight.
