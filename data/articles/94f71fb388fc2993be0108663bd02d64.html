It's almost time to lock in for what could be Prime Video's next TV hit. 56 Days, which stars Dove Cameron and Avan Jogia, is set to land on Amazon's streaming platform shortly, so you'll want to know when you can watch it.

In this guide, I'll tell you when the erotic crime thriller will launch on one of the world's best streaming services. You'll also find a full release schedule for program further down this page, so you can work out if it'll become appointment viewing every week or if you'll be able to watch it in one go on release day.

What is the launch time for 56 Days on Prime Video?

56 Days will make its Prime Video debut on Wednesday, February 18.

Developed by Lisa Zwerling and Karyn Usher, it's expected to drop at 12am PT / 3am ET in the US, which is when almost every new and returning Amazon TV Original has premiered on the service.

Live outside of the US and want to know what time it'll air on February 18 where you're based? Read on for more details:

  • US — 12am PT / 3am ET
  • Canada — 12am PT / 3am ET
  • UK — 8am GMT
  • India — 1:30pm IST
  • Singapore — 4pm SGT
  • Australia — 7pm AEDT
  • New Zealand — 9pm NZDT

What is 56 Days about?

Based on Catherine Ryan Howard's book namesake, 56 Days will see Cameron and Jogia play Ciara and Oliver, who enter an intense relationship after a seemingly chance encounter at a grocery store.

However, as their whirlwind romance gathers pace, it soon becomes apparent that one or both of them is hiding some big secrets. And, when the police break into Oliver's apartment one day and find a decomposing body, it sparks a chain of events that might lay out said secrets in full, and ruin their individual and collectives lives.

When will new episodes of 56 Days come out on Prime Video?

Fans of a binge watch persuasion won't need to wait for new chapters to drop every week. Indeed, 56 Days will be released in full on launch day, meaning you can watch all eight installments on February 18 if your heart desires.
