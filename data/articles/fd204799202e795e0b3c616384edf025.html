How to get the best value from Surfshark

Despite being a great cheap VPN, there are several steps you should take to ensure you maximise the value you get.

Most importantly, if you sign up, turn off auto-renewals. Surfshark's auto-renewal pricing jumps the cost massively, and there will always be a better VPN deal available — even from Surfshark itself. You can turn this off within your account in a matter of clicks, so make sure you do.

If you're unsure about signing up, Surfshark gives you more options than most. It offers a 30-day money-back guarantee, like you'd expect from any VPN, but you also have the choice of a 7-day free trial.

The 7-day free trial is a great option if you aren't sure how much you'll use a VPN and don't want to commit money early on. If you know you'll use a VPN but want to make sure Surfshark is right for you, then the money-back guarantee is a welcome backup choice.

Once you're signed up, make sure you get Surfshark downloaded on all your devices. Surfshark's app is available across almost every device, and you have unlimited device connections at your disposal, so why not make use of all of them?
