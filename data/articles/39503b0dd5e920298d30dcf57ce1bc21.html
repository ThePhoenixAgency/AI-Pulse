Frequently Asked Questions

Can I customize commission rates?

Yes. Each campaign has its own commission percentage and coupon discount percentage. You can create multiple campaigns with different rates.

Is RefWaffle GDPR compliant?

Yes. The core coupon-code approach needs no tracking at all — customers just type a code at checkout. The optional referral link snippet shows visitors a visible modal asking permission before storing anything. The visitor's click to apply the discount is their explicit consent, so no cookie consent banner integration is needed for affiliate tracking.
