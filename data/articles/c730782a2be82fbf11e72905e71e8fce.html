AirPods Pro 2, AirPods Pro 3, AirPods 4 just got another beta firmware update

Source: AppleInsider | Date: 2/12/2026 8:59:53 PM | Lien | Lang: EN

A new beta firmware for the AirPods Pro 2, AirPods Pro 3, and AirPods 4 has been released, though there are currently no details about its contents. A new beta firmware update is available for select AirPods models. Following the public release of iOS 26.3 on Wednesday, Apple has deployed a new beta firmware for select AirPods and AirPods Pro models. Thursday's developer beta increases the build number to 8B5034f, up from previous versions like the 8A5308b update. This beta software release is available for AirPods 4, AirPods Pro 2, and AirPods Pro 3. The software won't be made available for AirPods Max or other non-H2 devices.
