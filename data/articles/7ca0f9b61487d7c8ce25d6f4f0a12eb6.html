CREST CTF

Source: CTFtime | Date: Invalid Date | Lang: EN | Original Article

CREST CTF is a hybrid Capture The Flag competition organized by CREST Cyber Club.

This is a Jeopardy-style CTF featuring challenges across:
• Web Exploitation
• Reverse Engineering
• Cryptography
• Digital Forensics (DFIR)
• PWN
• Boot2Root
• Miscellaneous

The event follows a storyline titled “Operation: Ghost Mantis”, where participants investigate a simulated cyber breach affecting critical university systems.

This is a hybrid CTF with both onsite and online participation happening simultaneously. Onsite participants will compete from the venue, while online participants can join remotely.
