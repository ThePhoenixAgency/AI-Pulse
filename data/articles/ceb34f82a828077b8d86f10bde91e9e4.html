The OmniCTF Qualifiers are designed to be accessible for high school and university students, with a few harder challenges for experienced players.

TEAM SIZE for QUALS is unlimited.

TEAM SIZE for FINALS is up to 3 members.

These qualifiers will allow top 6 Romania-based teams to qualify for the on-site finals in Constanța, Romania (September - October).

NOTE (International teams - 3 slots only): If you attend the finals as an international team, we can provide accommodation for up to 2-3 days, as well as local (in-city) transport. We cannot fund flights or other travel expenses.
