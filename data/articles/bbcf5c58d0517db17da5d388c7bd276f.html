Why Mood2Know?

Mood2Know aims to become the world’s first emotional weather report.

Every day, millions of people experience emotions that remain invisible. Mood2Know was created to make this collective mood visible, in a simple and anonymous way.

By answering in just one click, you take part in a global experiment. No account, no explanation, no personal data — just a momentary feeling, shared as it is.

The goal is to reach 10,000 participants. Once this threshold is reached, Mood2Know will reveal a unique insight: a snapshot of the world’s emotional state at a given moment.

This emotional “weather” evolves constantly. Observing its changes helps put individual feelings into perspective and highlights the diversity of human experiences across places and time.

Mood2Know is not about analysis or diagnosis. It is a minimalist, open space designed for participation, curiosity, and awareness.

Mood needs you. Every response matters. The more people take part, the more meaningful this shared emotional picture becomes.
