Today's best Garmin Vivoactive 6 deal

If you're new to smartwatches and the best fitness trackers but don't want to commit to the Apple ecosystem, the Garmin Vivoactive 6 is a great alternative to the Apple Watch SE 3. iPhone or Android, it's ideal if you're after above-average fitness features but want a model that's easy to navigate, all at a reasonable price.

Unlike the Apple Watch SE lineup, the Vivoactive 6's battery life is it's biggest selling point. Garmin has a stellar suite of recovery metrics unlocked when you wear your watch overnight, like its Body Battery and Morning Report features.

The Smart Wake alarm tool is another feature we particularly enjoyed. Using haptic vibrations, this advanced feature wakes you up at the lightest point in your sleep cycle resulting in a more relaxed way to get up in the morning – so you can become the morning person you've always wanted to be.
