VPN deal of the week: Save $5 and get 3 months extra protection with the latest discount on Surfshark's One plan

Source: TechRadar | Date: 2/18/2026 5:03:08 PM | Lien | Lang: EN

There's just five days left to save on Surfshark's One Plan, meaning it's your last chance to save before the plan jumps back to its original $2.49 per month price.

The deal drops the overall cost of the 2-year plan to $61.83 from $67.23 — a $5.40, or 8% saving. While this is a relatively small reduction, the plan includes a significant number of features for the price.

If you're unsure whether Surfshark is the right fit, it's one of the few providers to offer both a 7-day free trial and a 30-day money-back guarantee.

This means if you're new to VPNs and don't want to make any financial commitment early on, you can use the 7-day free trial to put Surfshark through its paces. Alternatively, if you've used VPNs before and are debating the switch to Surfshark, the 30-day money-back guarantee gives you the time you need to ensure you've tried everything you need before committing.

Surfshark One is generally the plan I recommend because it balances a broad scope of security features without a massive price hike. However, if you don't need the antivirus or data monitoring, Surfshark's Starter plan remains even cheaper at $1.99 per month.

If you're after slightly stronger performance and are willing to pay the price for it, I'd still recommend NordVPN. NordVPN starts from $3.39 per month, but offers the best performance of any VPN we've tested across speeds, streaming unblocking, and security.
