Instant Halal & Haram Check

Free · In‑App Purchases · Designed for iPad

Halal Food AI Scanner helps you quickly check whether food products comply with Islamic dietary rules (halal). Point your camera at a barcode, paste an ingredient list, or upload a photo to get clear halal insights in seconds. KEY FEATURES: • Barcode Scanning: Instantly scan products and check halal status • AI Photo Analysis: Automatically read ingredient labels from photos • Text Analysis: Paste or type ingredient lists for fast verification • Detailed Explanations: Understand why items are halal, haram, or questionable • E-Code Detection: Identifies food additives and their halal status • Scan History: View and manage all your previous scans in one place • Multi-Language: Support for 16+ languages including Arabic, Turkish, English, and more HOW IT WORKS: Halal Food AI Scanner combines trusted food databases with advanced AI technology to analyze products. Simply scan a barcode, take a photo of ingredients, or paste text to receive instant halal or haram insights. PRIVACY & SECURITY: • No traditional accounts required using device-based identification • Photos are processed temporarily and never stored permanently • Your data remains private and you can clear scan history anytime • Transparent privacy policy available IMPORTANT NOTE: AI and photo analysis accuracy may vary depending on lighting and label quality. Always verify physical labels when in doubt. Designed for anyone who wants to make informed halal food choices quickly and confidently.
