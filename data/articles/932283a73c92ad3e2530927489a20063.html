How It Works

Galatea does the heavy lifting — you just pick a character and choose your agents

01

Enter Character

Give Galatea a character name or personality description — 'Sherlock Holmes', 'Grumpy Pirate Captain', anything you can imagine.

02

Select Source Type

Galatea identifies the personality source — fictional character, historical figure, archetype, or a fully original persona you describe.

03

AI Researches Traits

Galatea's research engine deep-dives into speech patterns, catchphrases, mannerisms, and characteristic quotes to build a personality profile.

04

You Refine & Target

Optionally add custom notes, then pick your coding agents — Claude Code, Cursor, Gemini CLI, or Codex. Galatea handles the rest.

05

Configs Generated

Galatea produces agent-specific configuration files — preview them with example interactions before committing.

06

Download & Drop In

One ZIP file. Drop the configs into your project directory. Your coding assistant now has personality.
