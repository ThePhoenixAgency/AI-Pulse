FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What data do you provide for each company?

Each company profile includes the full technology stack (frameworks, analytics, hosting, payment processors, marketing tools, and more), key contacts (CEO, CTO, VP Engineering, Head of Sales, etc.) with verified email addresses and LinkedIn profiles, plus company metadata like industry, location, employee count, founded year, and website.

How do you detect what technologies a company uses?

We analyze company websites, job postings, public repositories, and other public data sources to identify the technologies in use. Our data is updated weekly to reflect technology changes and new adoptions.

How are contact emails verified?

We use multiple verification methods including SMTP validation, domain verification, and cross-referencing with public professional profiles. Our database is updated regularly to remove bounced addresses and add newly verified contacts.

What types of contacts are available?

We focus on decision makers: CEOs, CTOs, VP Engineering, Head of Sales, Chief Revenue Officers, founders, and other C-level and VP-level executives. Each contact includes their title, verified email address, and LinkedIn profile when available.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes. Both monthly and annual plans can be cancelled anytime from your dashboard. No annual contracts, no cancellation fees. You retain access until the end of your billing period.

Is there a free plan?

Yes. Free users can browse the full directory, view company tech stacks, industries, locations, and sizes. Contact details (email addresses, LinkedIn profiles) are partially masked on the free plan. Upgrade to unlock full contacts, CSV exports, and saved lists.

How is StackWho different from BuiltWith?

BuiltWith and Wappalyzer show you what technologies a company uses -- but that's where they stop. StackWho goes further by also providing verified decision maker contacts (CEO, CTO, VP Engineering, Head of Sales) so you can actually reach out to the people behind the tech stack. Plus, we're significantly more affordable with no annual contracts required.
