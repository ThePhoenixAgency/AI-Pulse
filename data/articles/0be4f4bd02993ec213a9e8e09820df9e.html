Each paid user gets their own isolated server. Your conversations are encrypted end-to-end. We don't train on your data, share it with third parties, or even have access to it. Your data stays yours — period.

Can Cerebro send emails?

Yes. Cerebro can compose and send emails on your behalf, draft responses, and manage follow-ups — all directly from the chat interface. Connect your email account and let Cerebro handle the rest.

What AI model does Cerebro use?

Cerebro uses state-of-the-art language models, continuously updated to provide the best possible responses. Our architecture allows us to swap and upgrade models without any disruption to your experience.

Can I use Cerebro on mobile?

Yes. Cerebro works beautifully in any modern browser — desktop, tablet, or mobile. No app download required. Just open your browser and start chatting.
