What is the Micro-Habit Tracker?

A free, printable monthly habit tracking calendar inspired by Atomic Habits by James Clear. The idea is simple: pick your habits, print your calendar, hang it on your wall, and check off each day you complete a habit. Seeing an unbroken chain of checkmarks is one of the most powerful motivators for building lasting habits.

Why printable?

Because a physical sheet on your wall is harder to ignore than an app notification. No sign-up. No subscription. No ads. Just a clean calendar you print and use.

How it works

  1. Add your habits (e.g., "Read 10 pages," "Meditate 5 min," "No sugar")
  2. Pick a month and year
  3. Click "Print Calendar"
  4. Hang it up and start tracking

Inspired by Atomic Habits

This tool is built around core principles from Atomic Habits by James Clear:

  • Make it obvious — A calendar on your wall is impossible to ignore
  • Make it satisfying — Checking off a box gives you an immediate small reward
  • Don't break the chain — Visual streaks create their own momentum
  • Identity-based habits — Every checkmark is a vote for the person you want to become
