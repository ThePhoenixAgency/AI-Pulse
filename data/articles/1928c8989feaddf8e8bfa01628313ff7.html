I'm tired of looking for free APIs And I put this together

Source: Dev.to Open Source | Date: 2/17/2026 12:19:27 AM | Lien | Lang: EN

Hey everyone, ​We’ve all been there: you have a killer idea for a weekend project, but you spend more time navigating through "free tier" bait-and-switch pages—the ones that ask for your credit card "just for verification"—than actually writing code. I got frustrated with the process, so I started keeping a personal list of APIs that are genuinely free, easy to use, and perfect for building portfolio projects without the headache. Eventually, I realized this shouldn't just be a note on my laptop, so I turned it into a repo: Essential Free APIs for Devs. Why am I sharing this now? ​To be completely honest, the repo is brand new. It has zero stars and zero views right now. It’s just me and a README file. ​If you’re looking for data for your next app or just want to bookmark a useful resource, check it out here: https://github.com/NoxdevW/essential-free-apis-for-devs?tab=readme-ov-file Want to help out? ​If you have a favorite API that I missed, please open a Pull Request! And honestly, if you find the list useful, a star would mean the world to me—it helps me know I’m not just shouting into the internet abyss, haha.
