Apple TV keeps February 2026 fairly focused. On the movie side, there is one new Apple Original Film landing this month, and it leans into romance with a fantasy twist. If you came here looking for a longer movie slate, you will want to scan the new series arrivals too, because Apple TV has two major season premieres later in the month.

The headline release is Eternity, a romantic comedy set in an afterlife “junction” where the newly deceased get a limited window to decide where and with whom they will spend forever. It stars Elizabeth Olsen, Miles Teller, and Callum Turner, and it starts streaming on Friday, February 13, 2026.

After that, Apple TV shifts from films to returning series. The Last Thing He Told Me comes back with Season 2 on Friday, February 20, and Monarch: Legacy of Monsters returns for Season 2 on Friday, February 27.

Eternity

Eternity plays like a relationship story with a sci-fi or fantasy wrapper. After death comes “the junction,” and Joan has a week to choose her forever. The catch is emotional, not logistical. She reconnects with her long-time husband, but her first love also reappears, pulling her into a decision she never expected to make.

The cast does much of the heavy lifting. Elizabeth Olsen leads as Joan, with Miles Teller and Callum Turner anchoring the two sides of the choice. The tone stays in rom-com territory, but the setup gives it room to touch bigger themes like regret, timing, and the version of yourself you want to carry forward.

The Last Thing He Told Me Season 2

Season 2 picks up the show’s mix of family tension and suspense. Jennifer Garner returns as Hannah, and the story continues to circle a central question: what happens after you think the secrets are over, but the past refuses to stay buried. Apple is releasing the season weekly, so it will stretch well beyond February.

If you watched Season 1, you already know the series works best when it keeps Hannah and Bailey’s relationship at the center, even while the thriller plot pushes them from one problem to the next.

Monarch: Legacy of Monsters Season 2

Monarch: Legacy of Monsters comes back at the end of the month, again tying personal stories to large-scale Monsterverse stakes. Apple says Season 2 runs 10 episodes, with a weekly rollout through May 1, 2026.

Season 1 built its momentum by moving across timelines and showing how Monarch’s work ripples through families. Season 2 looks set to widen the scope, while keeping the same core cast and the same push-pull between secrecy and survival.
