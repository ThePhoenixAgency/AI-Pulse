Source: Hacker News Show | Date: 2/19/2026 3:31:06 PM | Lien | Lang: EN

Hey HN,

We built IPAware (https://ipaware.io) - an IP intelligence API that doesn't charge per request.

What it does:

- IP geolocation (country, region, city, coordinates, timezone)

- Threat detection (VPN/Proxy/Tor, risk scoring, datacenter detection)

- ASN/ISP data

- Clean JSON responses

Why we built this:

Every IP lookup service we tried had the same problem - you start small, your app grow and suddenly you're paying hundreds for what should be a commodity lookup. The metering anxiety is real. We wanted something we could integrate without worrying about the bill exploding.

How it works:

Flat monthly/yearly pricing. Pro plan is unlimited requests for $9.99/month or $99.90/year. Free tier gives you 5000 requests/month to try it out. Fair-use limits exist to prevent abuse, but no per-request metering.

Tech details:

- Edge delivery for low latency - REST API, returns JSON - No SDK required (works with curl, fetch, or any HTTP client) - Databases updated every night for accuracy - IPv4 and IPv6 support

Limitations to be upfront about:

- City-level accuracy varies by region (country/region is reliable, city is best-effort) - Fair-use applies on unlimited plans - this isn't for scraping the entire internet - Our threat detection algorithm is good but not perfect

Launch offer:

Your account gets a full month of unlimited Pro access with features that are not normally available to the free tier - no strings attached. At any point, you can decide to keep the Pro tier or switch to the free tier on the dashboard.
