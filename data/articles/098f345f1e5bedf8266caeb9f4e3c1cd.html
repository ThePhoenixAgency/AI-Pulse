Klimly — Accurate Weather Forecast from Multiple Sources

Source: Hacker News Show | Date: 2/14/2026 | Lang: EN | Original Article

Klimly combines weather data from OpenWeatherMap, WeatherAPI, Tomorrow.io, Pirate Weather, Open-Meteo, and Meteostat to deliver the most reliable forecast. Get real-time temperature, humidity, wind speed, precipitation probability, air quality index, pollen levels, and UV index for your location.

Features

  • Multi-source consensus weather — 6 providers cross-checked for accuracy
  • Hourly forecast with temperature, precipitation, and wind details
  • 7-day extended forecast with daily highs, lows, and conditions
  • Real-time air quality index (AQI) with PM2.5, PM10, O3, and NO2 levels
  • Pollen forecast for grass, tree, and weed allergens
  • AI-powered health insights: sleep quality, hydration, allergy risk, mood impact
  • Activity recommendations: running, cycling, hiking, photography, outdoor dining
  • Provider accuracy tracking with adaptive weighting
  • Sun vs shade feels-like temperature
  • Smart weather alerts

Weather Data Sources

Klimly aggregates forecasts from OpenWeatherMap, WeatherAPI, Tomorrow.io, Pirate Weather, Open-Meteo, and Meteostat. Each provider is independently tracked for accuracy, and more reliable sources receive higher weight in the consensus calculation.
