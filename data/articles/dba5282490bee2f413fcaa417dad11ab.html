Kakveda – Open Source Failure Intelligence

Source: Hacker News (nouveautés) | Date: 2/28/2026 10:03:52 AM | Lien | Lang: PT

Failure memory, not just logs

Kakveda is an open-source, event-driven platform that treats failures as first-class data. It adds failure memory, pre-flight warnings for known failure modes, recurring pattern detection, and system-level health scoring to modern LLM and distributed systems.

Get Started

Core Capabilities

  • Global Failure Knowledge Base (failure memory)
  • Pre-flight "this failed before" warnings
  • Recurring failure pattern detection
  • System health scoring over time
  • Event-driven microservices architecture
  • Runs locally via Docker Compose
