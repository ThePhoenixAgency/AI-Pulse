Let's start the weekend special by looking at a couple of freebies. The Epic Games Store began a big giveaway this week that carried two well-received detective games, namely Nobody Wants to Die and The Darkside Detective: A Fumble in the Dark.

From the duo, Nobody Wants to Die is a first-person adventure set in the future where you'll be recreating crime scenes and interviewing suspects to grab a serial killer. For those looking for a more old-school experience, they can jump into this The Darkside Detective entry, which is a retro-style point-and-click title featuring nine humorous supernatural cases.

The two giveaways will be active until February 19, which is when Return To Ash and STALCRAFT: X will become the next freebies.

The Humble Store's newest bundle is Valentine's Day themed, but with a horror twist. The Love You to Death Bundle carries nine items with a $12 price tag.

It carries Have a Nice Death, Ghost Watchers, Devour, Iron Lung, Lunacid, FAITH: The Unholy Trinity, The Bridge Curse 2: The Extrication, Life Eater, and Without a Dawn to keep as Steam keys.

The bundle has almost three weeks left on its counter before going away.
