iOS 26.4 redesigns Wallpaper Gallery

Source: 9to5Mac | Date: 2/16/2026 7:54:17 PM | Lien | Lang: EN

Apple just released the first iOS 26.4 beta, and it seems to feature a number of under-the-hood improvements. One change includes an overhauled Wallpaper Gallery system. With iOS 26.4, the Wallpaper Gallery has been redesigned and optimized for performance. The new system includes categories along the top; photo sections for Featured, Photo Shuffle, and Spatial Scenes; and familiar categories that can now be added and removed. For example, the new Wallpaper Gallery now lists sections for Weather, Astronomy, Emoji, and more alongside descriptions of each category. To see examples within each collection, you tap the Get button to actually add the options. Once loaded, you can browse the full collection or tap the checkmark button to remove the collection from the gallery. In addition to photo collections, the new Wallpaper Gallery includes preloaded collections for iOS 26 and the iPhone hardware-specific wallpapers that cannot be configured away.
