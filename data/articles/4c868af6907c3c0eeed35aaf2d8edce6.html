The Formula

Φ = I × ρ - α × S Where:
- I = (fidelity - 0.50) / 0.50 (normalized for 2-level system)
- ρ = T2/T1 ratio (coherence stability)
- S = readout error (entropy proxy)
- α = 0.1
- Threshold: Φ_c = 0.25
