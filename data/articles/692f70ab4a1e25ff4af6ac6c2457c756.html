The best fitness tracker deal for Presidents' Day is actually on a 6-year-old Fitbit – here's why

Source: TechRadar | Date: 2/16/2026 | Lang: EN | Original Article

The best Fitbits are the brand's more advanced devices, and not much more expensive than the Fitbit Inspire 2 I'm about to recommend to you. They have features the Inspire 2 does not, and sometimes these features are worth the price of admission, such as the ECG functionality – if you have heart issues, this is key, as it can alert you if your heart rate is too high.

However, the reason I'm recommending the older, less advanced Fitbit Inspire 2 is that it's an unbeatable value prosposition. Why, you ask? Because it's the last Fitbit device still on sale that comes with a whole year of Fitbit Premium – all other modern Fitbits net you just six months.

Fitbit Premium is a first-class fitness app offering guided workouts, recipes and meditation classes, more advanced ways to categorize and view your data, extra information such as sleeping heart rate, and features like the Daily Readiness Score. It costs $9.99/month or $119.88 annually.
